# ISO 42001

## ❓ What is it?

ISO 42001 is an international standard for building and governing an Artificial Intelligence Management System.

---

| 🔍 What does it do? | 🧠 Why do we need it? | ⚡ Why now? |
|--------------------|----------------------|------------|
| It provides a structured framework for how organizations develop, deploy, monitor, and govern AI systems across their lifecycle. | The standard was created because AI is now being used in high-risk business areas where poor oversight can lead to privacy violations, biased decisions, inaccurate outputs, legal exposure, regulatory scrutiny, and loss of public trust. | It matters now because many organizations are adopting AI quickly, but their governance, documentation, and risk controls have not kept pace with the speed of AI adoption. |
